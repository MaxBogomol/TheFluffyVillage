About Fabric/other loaders port

Right now mods are being developed for version 1.20 and their full release will take place on this exact version.

Further ports will be only on a higher version and only for one loader.

Porting mods is an incredibly labour-intensive process that takes as much time as developing the project.

These mods are being developed by 1 person, and porting them in parallel with main development will be beyond the possibilities of the developer, who alone work on the projects and their updates.

Why particles doesn't work correctly? Why I can only access a demo world?

Modern Optifine is horrible.

It breaks most of the graphics in mods and much more.

To avoid conflict, Fluffy Fur enables demo mode when Optifine is installed, enjoy!

It's better to use alternatives of Optifine, which are better in all aspects. It's recommended to use Embeddium.

No, this mod is fully independent project and it doesn't try to copy anything.

Wizard's Reborn - personal MaxBogomol's project which is being created on pure enthusiasm and is a reimagination of ideas from the author's favourite mods with with the addition of personal ideas.

How to progress in the mod?

In the mod there's a book called Arcanemicon, it will help you to explore it.

Most of the progression is linear, just read the articles in order and research mod's content.

>

How do I unlock spells? How do monograms works?

To unlock the spells, first of all you need knowledge points, which are given when unlocking new articles. If you don't have enough knowledge points, try to craft items etc.

On research page on the left side you can see the circle of monograms. You need to make the monogram chain of a given (or longer) length.

Monograms have their recipes: by connecting 2 shown monograms, you will receive the final one.

Connections inside the circle are calculated clockwise and visualised by the dots.

On the right page you can see the monograms itself and the recipes of choosed monogram.

How do I use the alchemy machine? How to generate steam?

To create steam, you need to supply water to Alchemy Furnace and fuel it. Steam will begin to produce, then it can be extracted from above.

To move fluids and steam, you need to create a path with pipes and connect an extractor to the storage. when the extractor is activated, the fluid/steam will start flowing through the pipes.

Alchemy is based on Alchemy Machine that creates fluids and items using other fluids, items, steam and wissen.

The Alchemy Machine is made of two blocks, the machine itself and the boiler on top.

The machine has three fluid inputs, here you need to pour the fluid needed for crafting. The boiler has one steam input and a fluid output, here you need to pour steam and take the finished fluid with the extractor. Also, for some crafts, wissen may be required, it is sent to the boiler.

To activate the craft, click on the machine with the functional mode of wissen wand.

You can find the approximate scheme of alchemy furnace and machine in the Arcanemicon.
